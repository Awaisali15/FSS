About Us

FSS Facilities Services Specialists, is a comprehensive service provider for various industries. With a diverse range of services including hotel and restaurant staff, cleaning, housemaid, and nursing staffs, FSS is committed to delivering exceptional service quality.

In addition, the company offers technical and mechanical services, property maintenance, general contracting, and construction services. FSS is dedicated to ensuring customer satisfaction by providing on-job and on-call training to staff, uniform supplies, and equipment and machinery supplies. The company also offers catering and event management services and specializes in pest control and landscaping.Choose FSS for reliable, efficient, and cost-effective service solutions.
